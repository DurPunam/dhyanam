Breathing Practice

Follow the guided breathing circle to regulate your breath and calm your mind. Inhale as it expands, exhale as it contracts.

Instructions:

  • Watch the circle expand and contract
  • Breathe in slowly for 4 seconds
  • Breathe out slowly for 4 seconds
  • Repeat for 5 minutes
